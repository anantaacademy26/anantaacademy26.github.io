  • Business ethics
  • Presentation skills

Business Email Writing

Email is a common form of communication in the business world. Learn how to write effective business emails with these tips:

  1. Use a professional and concise subject line.
  2. Address the recipient appropriately.
  3. Start with a polite greeting.
  4. Keep the email focused and to the point.
  5. Use clear and professional language.
  6. Proofread before sending.
  7. Include a professional signature.

Business Meeting Etiquette

Business meetings require proper etiquette to ensure productive discussions. Here are some important etiquette guidelines to follow:

  • Arrive on time.
  • Come prepared with necessary materials.
  • Listen actively and respectfully.
  • Avoid interrupting others.
  • Contribute constructively to the discussion.
  • Stay focused and avoid distractions.
  • Follow the meeting agenda.
  • Thank the participants at the end of the meeting.
